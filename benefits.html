Benefits of FACT ?

FACT: Bridge between the Farmer And Consumer with Technology and sustainable farming practices.

Existing Farming Practises and FACT Farming soution

In existing farm practices, there are some faults in farming methods. Due to which yield obtain has poor quality. Also, there is an Indirect value chain. So farmers don't get the actual profit they should get. The Fact farming solutions introduced improved farming methods, proper training to farmers, Which leads to a good quality yield. Also, the Fact farming solution introduces a direct value chain where Farmers will get the value they want for the crops.
