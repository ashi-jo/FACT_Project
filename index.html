    What is FACT ?

    Empowering farmers by eliminating harmful farming practices and ineffective agricultural supply chains in order to give them direct access to the end consumer.



    Vision

    A country where rural social entrepreneurs flourish and deliver greater sustainable financial and social impact

    Mission

    To empower farmers by creating livelihood opportunities through a sustainable ecosystem.

    Values

    Integrity & transparency Passion to serve Contagious sharing and collaboration Adaptability Empowered recreation

    Young farmer’s initiative in Nalgonda
    Rupani Ramesh with 75 cents he was able cultivate 40 different varieties of vegetable as multi-crop able make around 2.5 lacs as profit now he is striving towards educating farmers to learn mix row cultivation. He has also setup the "Sri Sedhya Rythu Seva Samstha" to train the young farmers on the farming methods of mixed row vegetable cultivation.
